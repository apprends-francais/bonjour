# Bonjour — Learn French 🇫🇷

A polished, offline French-learning app built for a beginner. No installation, no internet, no accounts — everything runs and saves on your Mac.

## How to open it

**Easiest:** double-click **`start.command`**. It opens Bonjour in your browser and keeps your progress saved. Keep the little Terminal window open while you study; close it when you're done.

**Also works:** double-click `index.html`. (Some browsers limit saved progress when opening files this way — `start.command` is more reliable.)

## What's inside

- **Learning Path** — a guided 6-unit beginner course. Each unit walks you through new words, a grammar point, verb conjugation, useful phrases, a role-play conversation, and a checkpoint quiz. Finish a unit to unlock the next.
- **Review** — spaced repetition (the proven method) resurfaces words right before you'd forget them. Grade each card *Again / Hard / Good / Easy* and the app schedules the rest.
- **Today's session** — one button on Home that chains your due reviews, new class words, and a quick quiz.
- **Practice** — free play, any topic: dictée (hear it, type it), a numbers trainer (0–1000 by ear), flashcards, listening, multiple-choice, conjugation (any verb, présent or passé composé, with an accent toolbar), match-up, and a tricky-words drill of what you keep missing.
- **Phone** — start.command shows an address to open Bonjour on your iPhone over the same wifi (phone progress is separate).
- **Grammar** — short, clear explanations of the essentials.
- **Phrasebook** — real phrases for cafés, travel, shopping, and meeting people, plus role-play dialogues.
- **Progress** — streak, level (A1–A2, matching your course), a 28-day activity chart, and per-topic mastery. Export or reset your progress anytime.

## Sound

Every French word and phrase can be read aloud (tap 🔊) using your Mac's built-in French voice — no internet needed.

Pick your voice and speaking speed under **Progress → La voix** (there's a Test button). Bonjour automatically prefers the clearest voice installed. For a big upgrade, download an enhanced voice once: **System Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → System Voice → Manage Voices… → French → Amélie (Enhanced), Thomas (Enhanced) or Audrey (Premium)**, then restart your browser.

## Earning progress

You earn XP for every review, quiz answer, and completed step. XP builds your level and feeds a daily goal and streak. It's all stored locally in your browser.
